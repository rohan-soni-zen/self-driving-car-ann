🚗 Self-Driving Car

What is this?

This is a simulation where 100 cars learn to drive by themselves using computer brains.

How does it work?

  • Blue cars are the cars learning to drive
  • Red cars are traffic obstacles
  • The cars use sensors to "see" the road and obstacles
  • They learn from their mistakes and get better over time

What you see:

  • Left canvas: The driving simulation
  • Middle canvas: The computer brain
  • Buttons: Save the best brain or start over

Watch and learn:

The best performing car (shown clearly) gets its brain saved. Other cars get slightly modified versions to try different strategies.
